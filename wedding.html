The Very Beginning
Little Kids, Big Feelings
We first crossed paths in pre-school — too young to understand what love was, but old enough to feel something. We shared classrooms through elementary and middle school, carrying quiet little crushes neither of us quite knew how to name.
Life Happened
She Moved Away
Then life did what life does — she moved away, and we each went on with our separate worlds. Years passed. But some people have a way of staying with you, even when distance says otherwise.
A Brief Reunion
Crossing Paths Again
Six years later, we briefly found ourselves in the same place at the same time. A glimpse. A reminder that someone was still out there. It wasn't the moment — but it planted something.
The Spark
A Soccer Game Changed Everything
Four years after that, a soccer game brought Lujens to her city. He reached out — "come hang, with the old crew." She came. And somewhere between old memories and new laughter, the spark that had been waiting all those years finally caught fire.
The Question
The Proposal
What started in pre-school, survived distance, time, and life — ended with him on one knee and her saying yes through tears of joy. Some stories just know where they're going.
